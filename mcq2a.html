What does the term 'Phishing' refers to?

C: A social engineering attack that tricks individuals into revealing sensitive information

Phishing attacks typically involve the use of fraudulent emails, messages, or websites that appear to come from a trustworthy source or reputable organization. The goal of the attacker is to deceive the recipient into providing personal information,such as passwords,credit card numbers.

Which of the following is the hacking approach where cyber-criminals design fake websites or pages for tricking or gaining additional traffic?

A: Pharming

Pharming is a strategy and approach used by cybercriminals to create phony web pages and sites in order to mislead users into giving over personal information such as login IDs and passwords.
