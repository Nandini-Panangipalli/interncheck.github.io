Level:5--Memory Card

Instructions

How To Play

  1. The cards are shuffled and are facing face down in rows on the web.
  2. The player turns over 2 cards.
  3. If the pictures match, the player keeps the cards and tries again for another match.
  4. If they do not match the cards are turned over again and the player goes for the next set of cards.
  5. The player must try to remember where they have seen cards, so they can use them to make a match.
  6. Remember:
    • Firstly you can select your set of cards randomly.
    • These cards are related to cyber security.
    • The overall round is about 45 secs and the total moves given are 15.
