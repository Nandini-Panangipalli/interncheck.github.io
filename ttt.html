Level:4--Word Puzzle

Instructions

How To Play

  1. It is a simple game.
  2. You will have to guess the given scrambled word correctly to get a point.
  3. You will get 3 chances and 20 secs timer to crack the scrambled word.
  4. If your score is 6 and above only you will be eligible to play the next level.
Points to Remember
  1. These scrambled words are cyber theme related.
  2. You have to give an answer whether it is right or wrong to go the next question.
  3. There are a few tricky questions where the word spacing is not properly given!..Careful with those words
