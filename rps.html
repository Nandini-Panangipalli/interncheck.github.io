Level:1--Rock Paper Scissors

Instructions

How To Play

Despite its underlying complexity, the game’s rules are straightforward. Players deliver hand signals representing rock, paper, or scissors, with the outcome determined by these three rules:

  1. Rock wins against scissors.
  2. Scissors win against paper.
  3. Paper wins against rock.

Remember to use the “Rock Paper Scissors Shoot” or “1, 2, 3, Shoot” method for a fair and synchronized start.





ROCK

The rock is when you place your hand into the form of a simple fist. The rock will beat scissors every time but will be beaten by paper.


PAPER

The paper is when you place your hand in an outstretched position.Paper will beat rock but will be beaten by scissors in no time.


SCISSORS

This is when you hold your fist with your index and middle finger pointing outwards in a V shape. Scissors will beat paper but rock beats scissors in no time.
